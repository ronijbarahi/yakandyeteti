Our Story

It started with homesickness

The thing we missed most about Kathmandu wasn't a place — it was a smell. Garlic and ginger hitting a hot pan before sunrise. A momo too hot to hold. Chai sweet enough to wake you up.

So we opened a little kitchen and started cooking the way our families do. Dough rolled thin, fillings seasoned by feel, nothing pre-made.
